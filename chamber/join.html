General Benefits Across Levels

  • Community Engagement:

    Opportunities to participate in community service initiatives and local advocacy projects.

  • Resources and Information:

    Access to valuable market research, business trends, and updates that can help inform business strategies.

  • Credibility:

    Being a member of the chamber lends credibility to your organization, showing commitment to the local economy and community.

Non-Profit Membership-No Fee

  • Networking Opportunities:

    Access to networking events tailored for non-profits to connect with other organizations, local businesses, and potential collaborators.

  • Advocacy:

    A voice in local issues affecting non-profits, including legislative advocacy and support for community initiatives.

  • Discounted Services:

    Reduced fees for chamber events and services, such as workshops and training specifically designed for non-profits.

Bronze Membership-$250

  • Basic Networking:

    General networking events to meet local business leaders and other members.

  • Listing in Directory:

    Your business listed in the chamber's member directory, increasing visibility among potential clients and partners.

  • Opportunities:

    Basic access to community and chamber events to promote your business, often through flyers or digital promotions.

Silver Membership-$450

  • Enhanced Networking:

    Invitations to exclusive member-only events, and opportunities for deeper connections with business leaders.

  • Marketing Exposure:

    Opportunities for increased visibility through sponsorship options at events, newsletters, and chamber website.

  • Educational Resources:

    Access to workshops, seminars, or training sessions that are relevant to your industry or business growth.

Gold Membership-$1000

  • Premium Networking:

    Priority access to all events, exclusive networking dinners, and opportunities to engage with high-profile speakers or business leaders.

  • Comprehensive Marketing:

    Superior advertising options, including prominent placement in chamber communications and the chance to host events.

  • Support Services:

    Additional resources such as business consulting, marketing support, and access to research and development resources specific to your industry.
