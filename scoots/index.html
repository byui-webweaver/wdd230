About us . . .

Welcome to Scoots, your go-to destination for discovering the enchanting island of Cozumel!
As a proud owner-operator company, we are dedicated to catering to a diverse clientele, including locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups.

At Scoots, we believe in providing friendly, hassle-free rental services for a variety of fun vehicles, including motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs. Whether you're looking to leisurely cruise the coastline or venture off the beaten path, we have the perfect options for you.

Join us and experience the carefree spirit of the island as you explore its stunning southern and eastern shores, far from the hustle and bustle of tourist hotspots.

Let your adventure begin!
